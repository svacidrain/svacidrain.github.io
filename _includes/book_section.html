A Risk Worth Taking

The Edge of Life

by Alex Carter

This is a true story, a sailor's journey like no other about chasing dreams, falling in love, disaster, tragedy and survival against insurmountable odds. The Edge of Life is a first-hand account. Left drowning in an ocean of guilt and regret, in a whirlwind of alcohol, illicit drugs and self-loathing, sometimes the best lessons are the hardest ones to learn.

This gripping account takes you on a voyage of redemption, survival, growth and acceptance across oceans, through storms, suicide, death, loneliness and letting go. The battle for survival is very real and is fought on all fronts. The push and pull between good and evil is constant.

At sea you can discover who you really are, but only through the love of others can you weather the storm. This isn't just a story about sailing, it's about running from expectations, love and the depths of your own mind. This is about life out there on the edge and every risk worth taking.
